About Us

Welcome to Restored Mauritius, where discarded wood is transformed into stunning, one-of-a-kind creations.

Founded by Eugene, a graphic artist turned craftsman, our journey began during the challenges of the Covid pandemic.

What started as a creative outlet has grown into a passion for crafting unique decorative pieces and simple furniture.

Inspired by the beauty of Mauritius, we work closely with local artisans to blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design, creating pieces that tell stories of renewal and creativity.

Thank you for supporting our mission to restore memories and craft art that lasts.
